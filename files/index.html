About me

Hi, I'm Jordan.

I spend most of my time reading, writing, and talking to people who are further along a path I'm curious about. This site is where that work lives — long-form posts, interview recordings, and a running list of resources I keep coming back to.

I started it because the best ideas I've come across rarely lived in one place. I wanted a single, honest home for the things I learn, so I can share them — and so I can find them again myself.

"Mission: to make good thinking easier to find, and good questions easier to ask."
